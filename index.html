About Us

This is the place where you come to ask all questions about nails. Why do my clients nails lift in a week? Why are they complaining about chipped nails just a few days after polishing? And for the love of God, why do my crystals fall off in a day? Or perhaps you are the average nail art loving Jane who wants to Ask A Nail Tech in training to practice on you.
Take a look through our profile pages and see the techs who are listed with us. After you make a selection, go ahead and see if they are free hook you up. Its a win win situation really-you get your nails done at a super economical price while a new nail tech gets to refine her craft.
